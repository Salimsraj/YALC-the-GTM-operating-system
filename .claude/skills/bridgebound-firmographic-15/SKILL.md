# Bridgebound Firmographic

Use this skill to build outreach around company-level changes and events.

## When This Skill Applies

- Growth or expansion outreach
- Funding or M&A timing
- Company-level business events that create urgency

## Pair With YALC

- Use `signals:fetch` and `signals:show` to confirm the event.
- Use `campaign:strategy` to decide if the signal is strong enough to launch.
