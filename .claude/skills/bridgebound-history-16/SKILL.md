# Bridgebound History

Use this skill to turn past relationship or prior-cycle history into outreach.

## When This Skill Applies

- Reviving closed-lost opportunities
- Re-engaging ghosts or stalled deals
- Turning previous meetings into a new campaign entry point

## Pair With YALC

- Use `lost-deal-revival-agent` when a public signal contradicts the original objection.
- Use `campaign:report` to monitor the re-engagement motion.
