# Bridgebound In Market

Use this skill to find active buyers who are already near a purchase decision.

## When This Skill Applies

- Targeting companies in a buying window
- Building competitive displacement campaigns
- Using recent events as the reason to reach out now

## Pair With YALC

- Use `signals:fetch` or `signals:enrich` to confirm the in-market signal.
- Use `campaign:create` once the list is prioritized.
