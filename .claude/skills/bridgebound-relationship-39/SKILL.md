# Bridgebound Relationship

Use this skill to turn warm relationships into a campaign entry point.

## When This Skill Applies

- Leveraging mutual connections or shared networks
- Building referral or warm intro campaigns
- Multi-threading into an account through known relationships

## Pair With YALC

- Use `campaign:strategy` to choose the angle.
- Use `personalize` to write the warm opener.
