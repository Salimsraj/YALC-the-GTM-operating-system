# Cold Email 4 Sequence

Use this skill to create a simple, effective cold email cadence.

## When This Skill Applies

- Building a new outbound sequence
- Planning first touch and follow-up messages
- Setting a compact email-only cadence

## What It Does

1. Designs Email 1 through Email 4.
2. Balances value, proof, and a clear CTA.
3. Keeps the sequence short enough to stay readable and testable.

## Pair With YALC

- Use `email:create-sequence` to generate the workflow.
- Use `email:send` to launch it.
- Use `campaign:monthly-report` or `campaign:report` to review performance.
