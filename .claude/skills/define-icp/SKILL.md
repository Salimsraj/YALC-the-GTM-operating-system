# Define ICP

Use this skill to translate a market into a target profile with explicit fit rules.

## When This Skill Applies

- Defining an ICP from a customer segment or revenue target
- Building scoring criteria or tier systems
- Translating firmographic, technographic, and behavioral constraints into a target list

## What It Does

1. Clarifies the target market, geography, size, and revenue bands.
2. Captures must-haves, nice-to-haves, and disqualifiers.
3. Produces a simple tiering model that downstream list-building and qualification can use.

## Pair With YALC

- Use `campaign:strategy` before a new outbound launch.
- Use `leads:qualify` once the ICP is finalized.
- Use `signals:fetch` or `signals:similar` to expand the account universe.
