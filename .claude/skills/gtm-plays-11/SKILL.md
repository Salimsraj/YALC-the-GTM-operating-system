# 11 GTM Plays

Use this skill to pick the right GTM motion for a campaign.

## When This Skill Applies

- Choosing a play for a segment or signal
- Turning a trigger into a campaign concept
- Comparing inbound, outbound, and bridgebound motions

## What It Does

1. Maps the request to one of the available plays.
2. Suggests the best channel and persona mix.
3. Hands off to the campaign or personalization workflow.

## Pair With YALC

- Use `campaign:strategy` to pre-flight the idea.
- Use `campaign:create-sequence` or `email:create-sequence` to execute it.
