# LinkedIn Campaign Complete

Use this skill to build a LinkedIn outreach motion from targeting to follow-up.

## When This Skill Applies

- Planning a connect-plus-DM flow
- Reviewing LinkedIn targeting strategy
- Building a full LinkedIn campaign sequence

## What It Does

1. Aligns target selection with the message.
2. Chooses the right sequence structure.
3. Keeps the outreach human and easy to review.

## Pair With YALC

- Use `launch-linkedin-campaign` to stage a full flow.
- Use `campaign:dashboard` to review live campaign status.
