# LinkedIn Success Factors

Use this skill as a pre-launch checklist for LinkedIn campaigns.

## When This Skill Applies

- Before sending requests or DMs
- When a LinkedIn campaign underperforms
- When you want a quick quality gate for LinkedIn execution

## What It Does

1. Checks audience fit.
2. Checks message clarity and relevance.
3. Checks cadence and follow-up discipline.

## Pair With YALC

- Use `launch-linkedin-campaign` after the checklist passes.
- Use `track-campaigns` to monitor replies and sequence progress.
