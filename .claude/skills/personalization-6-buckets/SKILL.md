# Personalization 6 Buckets

Use this skill to decide what research deserves a personalized opener.

## Buckets

1. Self-authored content
2. Engaged content
3. Self-identified traits
4. Junk drawer details
5. Background-centric signals
6. Company-level information

## Pair With YALC

- Use `research` or `research-prospect` to gather source material.
- Use `personalize` to convert the best bucket into a message.
- Use `send-cold-email` only after the hook is approved.
