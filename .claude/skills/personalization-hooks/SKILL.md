# Personalization Hooks

Use this skill to turn research into a usable first line.

## When This Skill Applies

- Writing openers based on a post, article, or company signal
- Choosing between verbatim and conceptual relevance
- Building a hook from a single piece of evidence

## What It Does

1. Chooses the strongest available data point.
2. Decides whether to quote directly or paraphrase.
3. Produces a short opener that fits the campaign tone.

## Pair With YALC

- Use `personalize` for the full message.
- Use `campaign:create` or `email:create-sequence` for sequence-level application.
