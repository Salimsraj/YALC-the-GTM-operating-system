# Write Outbound Messaging

Use this skill when you want to build a customized messaging framework for your outbound campaigns.

## What This Skill Does

1. **Asks 12 strategic questions** about your business, ICP, and messaging goals
2. **Builds a custom message template** based on your answers
3. **Generates 5 personalization angle variations** for different buyer personas
4. **Outputs ready-to-use messaging** you can immediately deploy

## Questions You'll Answer

### Your Business (3 questions)
- What do you do / what problem do you solve?
- Who is your ideal customer profile?
- What's your main differentiator or proof point?

### The Pain Point (3 questions)
- What's the primary pain/problem your customers face?
- What's the consequence if they don't fix it?
- What false assumption do most people have about this problem?

### Your Proof (2 questions)
- What's your strongest proof point? (metric, case study, result)
- What timeframe or cost does it take to achieve results?

### Tone & Style (2 questions)
- How should the tone feel? (founder-to-founder, professional, casual, contrarian?)
- Any specific phrases or approaches you want to avoid?

### Personalization (2 questions)
- What are the 3-5 different buyer personas you're targeting?
- Do you want separate messaging angles for each, or one template with variations?

## Output

You'll get:
- 1 core message template
- 5 personalization variations (if multiple personas)
- Copy-paste ready format
- Subject line suggestions

## When to Use

- Starting a new outbound campaign
- Building a cold email sequence
- Creating messaging frameworks for your sales team
- Testing different angles for your ICP
- Refining your value proposition messaging

## Time to Complete

~5-10 minutes to answer questions
~2 minutes to generate messaging
